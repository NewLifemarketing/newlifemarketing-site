Our story

Started In A Basement Belief, Not A Boardroom

NewLife Marketing was founded in 2022 in Barrie, Ontario, by Cruz Lee, on one belief: a small business deserves better than a cookie-cutter strategy and a junior account manager. It started with Cruz building campaigns for local businesses, camera in hand — and grew into a full digital marketing agency in Ontario.

The rule then is the rule now — the work has to pay for itself. Today that same crew films, edits, runs the ads and builds the CRM for clients like Elite Cards Toronto, DMT Building Group and Chicken On The Run — and has taken the cameras from Toronto to Aruba, Tampa and Washington DC.
