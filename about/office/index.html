Straight answers

Visiting FAQ

The practical questions before you drive out — parking, appointments and on-location shoots.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — the office runs by appointment so someone's there to actually sit down with you. Book a strategy call and we'll set a time; drop-ins can catch us mid-shoot.

Where do I park?

There's free on-site parking, including wheelchair-accessible spots right by the entrance — just pull in.

Can you shoot at my location instead of the office?

Absolutely — that's how most of our work happens. Our crew brings the gear to your shop, job site or restaurant across Barrie, Simcoe County and Ontario. The office is for strategy, editing and meetings.

Where exactly are you?

70 Bearberry Rd, Minesing, ON L9X 2E1 — about ten minutes west of downtown Barrie, in Minesing. The map above has a live pin.
