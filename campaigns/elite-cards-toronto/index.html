The challenge

Where They Started

Elite Cards needed a scalable acquisition engine — one that captured the collectors already searching for cards, sealed product and singles, while creating new demand with short-form creative and collector-focused targeting. And it had to grow beyond one campaign and one city.

Starting point — Nov 2025 — launch: $545 spend → $11.9K reported value.

What we ran

The Play, Step By Step

The hook

Live-sale clips and reels shot on the shop floor — the exact energy collectors scroll for.

The audience

Segmented into new, existing and engaged buyers, then widened Toronto → Montreal → BC.

The offer

Sealed product, singles and live breaks — matched to what each audience was already hunting.

The funnel

Google Search captured active intent, Meta created new demand — our creative fed both.

The climb

The Growth, Month By Month

Behind the ad accounts, the store itself tells the fuller story: Elite Cards' Shopify did CA$4.9M in sales across 24,080 orders in the year to July 2026 — up 110% year over year. The paid engine (Google + Meta) drove $1.49M+ in reported conversion value on $17.1K of spend to help get it there.
