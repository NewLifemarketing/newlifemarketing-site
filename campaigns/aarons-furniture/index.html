The Campaign At A Glance

Industry: Furniture, electronics & appliancesServices: Meta lead generation, filmed ad creativeClient since: May 2026 — launched on the Northern Ontario road trip

The challenge

Where They Started

Aaron's sells considered purchases — furniture, appliances, electronics, flexible payment plans — where the sale usually starts with a conversation. The store team needed qualified local prospects to talk to, and the partnership had just been launched in person on the Thunder Bay road trip. The first-month goal was modest: 10 qualified leads.

What we ran

The Play, Step By Step

A dual Meta lead system, both arms fed by ad creative we filmed at the store. A Messenger conversation campaign put prospects straight into live chats with Aaron's sales team — where staff could answer questions and qualify buyers in real time. An instant lead-form campaign captured contact details inside Meta for follow-up. Two ways in, one pipeline of local buyers.
