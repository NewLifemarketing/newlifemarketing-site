The problem

Most Google Ads Money Is Quietly Wasted

The average business owner turns on Google Ads, watches the budget disappear into broad keywords and untracked clicks, and concludes “ads don't work.” The ads work fine — the setup didn't. No conversion tracking, no negatives, no landing page, no one minding it after launch.

Google makes it effortless to spend money and genuinely hard to spend it well. That gap — between spending and managing — is where your competitors are beating you, and it's exactly the gap we close.

What it is

Google Ads, In Plain English

Google Ads puts your business in front of people at the exact moment they're searching — for a plumber, a product, a service, a place to eat. You only pay when someone clicks (or calls). The catch: it spans seven very different campaign types across Search, YouTube, Shopping, Maps and the web. Picking the right one — and running it against the right number — is the whole game.

The campaign types

Every Google Ads Campaign, And When It's Right For You

“Google Ads” isn't one thing — it's seven different machines. Running the wrong one is how budgets get burned. Here's each type in plain English, and how we run it.

Search campaigns

When someone is actively searching for what you sell

Text ads at the very top of Google results for high-intent searches like “emergency plumber Barrie.” We do the keyword research, set match types, write the ad copy, build negative-keyword lists so you stop paying for junk clicks, and wire up conversion tracking so every call and form is counted.

Performance Max

When you want Google's AI to find buyers across every surface

One campaign that runs across Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Discover and Maps. We build the asset groups (headlines, images, video, logos), feed Google strong audience signals, and fence it in with the right data so the AI optimizes toward real revenue — not just cheap clicks.

Display & Remarketing

When you want to stay in front of people who already know you

Banner ads across the two-million-site Google Display Network. The heavy lifter here is remarketing: showing your ad to people who visited your site or added to cart but didn't convert — the cheapest conversions you'll ever buy.

Shopping campaigns

When you sell products online

Product ads with image, price and store name right in the results. We set up and clean your Merchant Center feed, optimize product titles and attributes, and structure campaigns by margin and best-sellers. Pairs with our E-Commerce & Retail playbook.

YouTube & Video

When the story needs motion to land

Skippable and non-skippable video ads targeted by intent, interest and audience. This is our edge — we produce the ad in-house with our cinema crew, so you're not running a slideshow against competitors with real film.

Demand Gen

When you want scroll-stopping discovery at scale

Visually rich image and video ads across YouTube, Discover and Gmail — the closest Google gets to a social feed. Great for demand creation when search volume alone isn't big enough to hit your growth targets.

Local & Local Services Ads

When you need calls, directions and booked jobs nearby

Campaigns tied to your Google Business Profile that drive calls and directions, plus Local Services Ads (the “Google Guaranteed” pay-per-lead units) for eligible trades like HVAC, plumbing and electrical. We handle eligibility, verification and the review signals that decide who shows. Feeds our Home Services & Trades work.

Straight answers

Google Ads FAQ

The real questions owners ask before spending on Google — answered honestly, including the budget one.

How much should I budget for Google Ads?

Two numbers: ad spend (paid to Google) and management. For most Barrie & Simcoe County local businesses we set your spend high enough for Google to gather data and optimize — the right level differs by market. Pricing is custom per client and package, so we don't publish set rates; you'll get a real number for your market on the call, not a vague “it depends.”

How is this different from just boosting posts or running it myself?

Boosting optimizes for likes; a managed account optimizes for booked jobs and sales, with conversion tracking, negative keywords and weekly search-term mining behind it. The platform is easy to spend money on and hard to spend it well — that gap is the whole job.

Which campaign type do I need?

It depends on intent. If people are already searching for you, Search and Local. If you sell products, Shopping. If you need demand creation, YouTube, Demand Gen and Performance Max. On the strategy call we map your goals to the right mix instead of defaulting to one.

What are Local Services Ads and can I use them?

LSAs are the pay-per-lead “Google Guaranteed” units at the very top for local trades — you pay per lead, not per click. Eligibility depends on your trade and passing Google's verification. We handle the application, verification and the reviews that decide ranking.

How soon will I see results?

Search campaigns can produce leads in the first week. Performance Max and Shopping need 2–4 weeks of learning data before they stabilize. Real optimization gains show over the first 60–90 days as the account accumulates conversion data.

Do you build the landing pages too?

Yes — sending paid traffic to a weak page wastes the spend. We build or recommend landing pages designed for the click, wired into your CRM so no lead is lost. See CRM Systems.

Who owns the ad account?

You do. We build inside your Google Ads account so you keep all the history, data and assets if we ever part ways. No hostage accounts.

What do I get in reporting?

A monthly report in plain English: what you spent, how many leads or sales it produced, and your cost per result — plus what we changed and what's next. You'll never need a data-science degree to read it.
