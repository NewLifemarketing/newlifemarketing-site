The problem

Leads Don't Usually Get Lost Loudly — They Just Slip

A missed call no one calls back. A form fill that sits for a day. A quote that never gets a follow-up. A past customer who would have bought again if anyone had reached out. None of it feels like a crisis — it feels like a normal week — which is exactly why it's so expensive.

Most small businesses run their pipeline on sticky notes, memory and good intentions. The leads you paid to generate leak out the bottom faster than you can replace them. A CRM buildout plugs the leaks — automatically.

What it is

CRM Systems, In Plain English

A CRM buildout is us building the whole system that catches a lead the moment it arrives, follows up instantly and consistently, moves it toward a booked job, and then turns that customer into a review and repeat business — with as much of it automated as possible.

Think of it as the operating system for your sales. Ads and content bring people to the door; the CRM is what makes sure someone always answers, follows up, and closes — even when you're on a job site. We build it on our platform and run it for you.

The system we build

One System, Built Around Your Lead Lifecycle

We don't hand you software and wish you luck. We build and run the whole lead-to-customer system for you, across five stages — capture, nurture, close, evangelize, reactivate.

① Capture — every lead in one place

So no enquiry ever lands somewhere no one checks

We build the intake: lead-capture forms, funnels and landing pages, a website chat widget with Conversation AI, call tracking, and inbound SMS & social DMs unified into one inbox — plus QR codes and your Google/Meta ad lead-forms wired straight in. Every lead from every source routes into one CRM record.

② Nurture — instant, consistent follow-up

Because speed and persistence win deals, not luck

Visual sales pipelines, automated workflows, booking calendars with reminders, and one conversation stream across SMS, Messenger, Instagram DM, WhatsApp and live chat. We build the follow-up so every lead is worked in seconds and never forgotten.

③ Close — remove the friction to paid

So “interested” turns into “booked and paid”

Lead scoring, estimates and proposals, invoicing, payments (Text-2-Pay, Tap-2-Pay), order forms and upsells, and paid calendars. We take the drag out of the step where most deals quietly die.

④ Evangelize — turn customers into a reputation engine

Because reviews and referrals compound for free

Automated review requests by SMS/email/WhatsApp with feedback-gating (happy → public Google review, unhappy → private form), review dashboards, AI review replies, and referral/affiliate tracking. Your reputation grows on autopilot.

⑤ Reactivate — pull sales from contacts you already have

Because your database is money left on the table

Broadcast campaigns, smart-list segmentation, birthday and seasonal automations, and database-reactivation sequences. We wake up dormant leads and past customers to pull repeat business out of the list you already own.

The automations we build

The Automations That Do The Work For You

This is the heart of a buildout. We design each automation around your exact sales process using triggers (new lead, booking, pipeline-stage change, call status), actions (send SMS/email, drop a voicemail, wait, update fields, notify you) and branching logic. A few of the ones we build most:

Missed-Call Text-Back

Trigger: a call you miss

The second a call goes unanswered, the system fires a personalized text back — so the lead who would have called your competitor next stays yours.

Speed-to-Lead

Trigger: a new lead from any source

An instant text and email go out within seconds of a form fill or ad lead. Speed-to-lead is the #1 factor in whether a lead converts — we make it automatic.

Appointment Reminders

Trigger: a booked appointment

An email + SMS + optional voicemail sequence runs before the appointment to cut no-shows and protect your calendar.

Review-Request Automation

Trigger: a deal marked “Customer”

After a wait timer, the system asks for a review — with feedback-gating so happy customers go to Google and unhappy ones reach you privately first.

No-Answer Nurture Drip

Trigger: an outbound call with no answer

Unreached leads drop into a timed text/email drip so nobody goes cold while you're on the tools.

Database Reactivation

Trigger: a segmented broadcast you launch

Smart-list campaigns to dormant leads and past customers — a promo, a check-in, a seasonal offer — to turn an idle list into booked jobs.

AI Voice / Chat Handling

Trigger: an inbound call or message, 24/7

AI answers, qualifies, updates the CRM record and hands off to a workflow or a live rep — so after-hours leads are captured instead of lost.

Straight answers

CRM Systems FAQ

The questions owners ask before we build their system — ownership, cost, timeline and whether it replaces what they already pay for — answered straight.

What exactly is a CRM buildout?

It's us designing and building your entire lead-to-customer system in one place: where every lead lands, how they're automatically followed up with, how deals move to a close, and how customers become reviews and repeat business — all automated, tracked, and run for you.

Do I need to be technical to use it?

No. We build it, we run it, and we train your team on the simple day-to-day parts (checking the inbox, moving a deal, reading the dashboard). The complex automation work stays on our side.

Can it replace the tools I already pay for?

Usually, yes — that's a big part of the value. It consolidates your CRM, funnels, email, SMS, scheduling, payments and reviews into one system, so you can often cancel several separate subscriptions.

Will my leads and contacts be mine?

Completely. It's your own branded account and your data is yours — you keep the contacts, the history and the system. Nothing is held hostage.

How long does a buildout take?

A focused core system (pipelines + the key automations) is typically live in 2–4 weeks. More complex builds with a full AI layer and multiple pipelines take longer — we scope it honestly on the call.

Does it integrate with my ads and website?

Yes — that's the point. Your Google and Meta ad lead-forms, website forms, funnels and chat all feed the same CRM, so every lead lands in one place and gets worked the same way.

What's the investment?

Most buildouts run as a one-time build plus a monthly management fee, both scoped to complexity and how much we run for you — so it's different per client and package and we don't publish set rates. We'll give you a real number for your setup on the call.

Do you manage it after launch, or just build it?

Both are available. Most clients have us build it and then keep managing and optimizing it as real leads flow through — but if you'd rather run it yourself after training, that's fine too. You own it either way.

Is it really my own branded system?

Yes — your logo, your login, your account. Your team and customers experience it as your system, not a third-party tool with someone else's name on it.
