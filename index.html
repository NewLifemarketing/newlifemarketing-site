Good questions

Questions Owners Ask Us

Straight answers before you ever book a call.

Where are you based, and who do you work with?

Our office and studio are in the Barrie area. We work with businesses across Simcoe County and all of Ontario — and we travel for productions when a project calls for it. Most of the work is done remotely after the kickoff, so location is never a barrier.

Do you really have an in-house film crew?

Yes. The video and photo you see across this site was shot by our own crew — the same people you meet on the strategy call are the ones behind the camera. That's the part most agencies outsource, and it's why our creative actually looks like your business.

What makes NewLife different from a typical agency?

One small senior team owns your account end to end — strategy, ads, creative and CRM under one roof, with one point of contact who's accountable for the results. You're never handed off to a junior or told "that's a different department."

How much does it cost?

Every engagement is scoped to the business — the channels you need, the volume of creative, and the goals we're chasing all move the number, so there's no one-size price list. Book a free strategy call and you'll leave with a clear plan and a straight answer on investment.

How soon will I see results?

Paid media can start producing leads and sales within the first few weeks, while SEO and organic content compound over months. We don't sell guarantees — we set honest expectations on the call and report on real numbers, not vanity metrics.

What happens on the free strategy call?

You'll talk to Cruz or Keegan directly — not a sales rep. We look at where you are, where you want to be, and you leave with a written growth plan you can keep whether you hire us or not.
