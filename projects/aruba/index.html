The brief

Why We Went

The client: Casa Bella y Dushi, a portfolio of luxury vacation rentals in Aruba. We travelled across the island to build a complete visual content ecosystem — not just the properties, but the lifestyle, scenery and experiences that make Aruba one of the world's most sought-after destinations. Commercial-style production, aerial cinematography, photography and immersive property tours, all designed to help future guests experience Aruba before they even arrive.

The challenge

The properties were exceptional, but standard listing photos couldn't communicate the full experience of staying in Aruba. Potential guests needed to see more than bedrooms and amenities — they needed the beaches, excursions, atmosphere and lifestyle that make the destination unique. The goal: content that sold the experience, not just the accommodation.
