Straight answers

Local Business Marketing FAQ

What local owners ask before working with us — measurement, filming, budget and the map pack — answered straight.

How do you actually measure foot traffic?

Through the signals we can track honestly — offer redemptions, reservation and booking volume, calls and direction requests from your Google profile, and reach in your area — plus your own read on busier days. We won't invent a precise “+X visitors” number we can't verify.

Do you film at my location?

Yes — that's the point. Storefronts sell on atmosphere, so our crew shoots on-site: the food, the product, the space and your team. See Creative Production.

What's the map pack and why does it matter?

It's the block of three local businesses with a map at the top of “near me” searches. Most local discovery happens there, so we optimize your Google Business Profile and reviews to get you in it.

How much should a local business budget?

Local campaigns can run leaner than national ones, but the right level is different per storefront and package — so we'll size ad spend plus content to your goals on the call rather than quote a set rate.

Do you run promotions and events?

Yes — seasonal offers, launches and events are some of the best foot-traffic drivers, and we turn them into both content and paid campaigns.

Is organic social worth it, or just ads?

Both. Organic builds the local community that trusts and returns; ads put a reason to visit in front of new people. Together they compound.

Do I own my accounts and content?

Always — your profiles, your footage, your community. We build inside your assets.
