Straight answers

Health and Wellness Marketing FAQ

The questions gyms, studios and wellness businesses ask — offers, filming, trial conversion and budget — answered straight.

What kind of offer works best?

A low-friction trial, challenge or intro offer almost always outperforms “join now” — it gets people in the door, where your space and coaches do the selling. We build the offer and the funnel around it.

Do you film at my gym or studio?

Yes — energy is everything in fitness, so we shoot classes, transformations and real members on-site. See Creative Production.

How do you get trials to actually convert to memberships?

The CRM. Every lead is captured and run through an automated trial-to-membership nurture with reminders and follow-up, plus retargeting — so trials don't fizzle and no-shows drop.

Can you run campaigns for specific programs?

Yes — kids' programs, new-year pushes, seasonal challenges. We target the right segment in the right radius so you reach people close enough to show up (Santos runs kids'-program campaigns across a 90km radius).

What should I budget?

It's different per studio and package, so we don't quote a set rate — we'll size ad spend plus content to your class capacity and goals on the call.

Does this work for salons, spas and clinics too?

Yes — the same offer + content + booking-CRM system fills appointment books, not just class schedules.

Do I own the content and accounts?

Always — your footage, your ad accounts, your member data.
