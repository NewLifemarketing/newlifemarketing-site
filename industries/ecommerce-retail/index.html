The problem

Rising Ad Costs, Flat Sales, And Reporting You Can't Trust

Ad costs keep climbing, one winning ad gets fatigued and there's no system to find the next one, and the agency's dashboard says one thing while Shopify says another. Meanwhile the customers you already paid to acquire never hear from you again.

Growing a product brand isn't about a single lucky ad — it's a system: constant fresh creative, a real funnel, and owning the customer relationship so revenue compounds.

How we market it

How Our Ecommerce Marketing Agency Grows Your Brand

One connected system — ads → creative → store → email/SMS follow-up → tracked, store-verified revenue. Here's how each piece works for a product business.

Paid social as a full funnel

Meta — prospecting → retargeting → retention

Catalog/dynamic product ads and UGC-style product reels find cold buyers; retargeting closes the browsers and cart-abandoners; retention keeps them buying. The offer and creative are matched to each stage of the funnel, not one ad blasted at everyone.

Google Shopping + high-intent Search

Catch the people already trying to buy

Merchant Center feed setup and product-feed optimization put your products in Shopping results, while Search captures branded and high-intent queries. This is the cheapest revenue in e-commerce because the intent is already there.

Creative volume — the real ROAS lever

Because on a feed, the ad IS the targeting

We produce a steady stream of product reels, lifestyle, UGC-style and offer creative in-house, then test relentlessly. The brands that win aren't the ones with the biggest budget — they're the ones feeding the algorithm the most winning creative. Tie-in: Creative Production.

Email & SMS flows

The margin most stores leave on the table

Abandoned-cart, welcome, post-purchase, win-back and launch flows — automated in the CRM so repeat revenue compounds without more ad spend. Owning the customer relationship is how ROAS actually stays healthy.

Store SEO & conversion

Turn the traffic you're paying for into orders

Product- and category-page SEO for free organic traffic, plus conversion improvements on the store itself — because a 1% lift in conversion rate lifts every channel's ROAS at once.

Straight answers

Ecommerce Marketing Agency FAQ

The questions product brands ask before scaling with us — ROAS, budget, creative and ownership — answered honestly.

What ROAS should I expect?

It depends on margin, price point and how much creative we can test — a 2x ROAS can be great for a high-margin brand and thin for a low-margin one. We optimize toward profitable return for your specific numbers, and we'll set a realistic target on the call rather than promise a fantasy multiple.

How much ad budget do I need?

Enough for Meta and Google to gather data and optimize — too little and there's no signal to work with. The right level is different per store, margin and package, so we'll recommend a number that fits your margins on the call rather than a one-size figure.

Why does creative matter so much?

On a social feed the creative is the targeting — the algorithm finds buyers based on who responds to the ad. More winning creative = more scale. That's why we produce it in-house at volume.

Do you build the email and SMS flows too?

Yes — abandoned-cart, welcome, post-purchase and win-back flows are some of the highest-ROI work we do, and they run in the same CRM as everything else.

Do you work with in-store retail, not just online?

Yes. We drive online revenue and, for shops with a physical location, foot traffic too — see our Local & Storefront playbook, which we often blend with this one.

Can you fix my store, or only run ads?

We do conversion-rate work on the store itself — sending paid traffic to a page that doesn't convert just burns budget, so improving the store is part of protecting your ROAS.

Do I own my accounts and data?

Always. Ad accounts, store, email list and creative are yours — we build inside your assets, never hostage ones.
